OUR MISSION

Mom’s Gourmet is the ideal spot to be greeted if you are a student, traveller or newcomer to the city looking for a cosy treat. So join us and enjoy a homely delight.

All food prepared in homes
Mom’s gourmet targets on delivering home-like food to everyone, specially for students and travelers who move to a new city
Cheap Prices
As all the food is prepared by mothers and other women at home, and delivered directly to you, so there are no extra charges for dining, services, etc.
Monthly Packages Available
You can get the monthly package and everyday, the food will be delivered to you so you need not to order it daily, also the plans offer less pricing than individual ordering.
A side hustle for Moms
Any women can join as a food provider, and can work as much as she wants, and will be given good amounts of income on the basis of orders and other bonuses
Women Empowerment
Our mission is to empower all the moms so they have their own financial stability and they can do something for their own benefits.
Workflow Builder
Regular orders and reviews will build a positive workflow among all our contributor women and they will have good amount of experience of working in a managed environment.
